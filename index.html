How to play with your eyes
  • Aim: move your gaze over a cell — it highlights.
  • Reveal: hold your gaze on a cell (dwell) until the ring completes.
  • Flag: do a deliberate, slightly-long blink (both eyes) on the highlighted cell.
  • Natural quick blinks are ignored, so you won't flag by accident.

No camera? It also plays with mouse (left = reveal, right = flag) and keyboard (arrows, Enter, F).
