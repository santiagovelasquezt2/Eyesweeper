How to play with your eyes
  • Aim: move your gaze over a cell — it highlights and the cursor snaps to it.
  • Reveal: hold your gaze on a cell (dwell) until the ring completes.
  • Flag: blink (or wink) on the highlighted cell.
  • Natural quick blinks are ignored, so you won't flag by accident.

No camera? Mouse (left = reveal, right = flag) and keyboard (arrows, Enter, F) always work.
